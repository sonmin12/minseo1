Rule

Both teams in the game have 11 players on the field. All players except the goalkeeper cannot use their hands except for the drawing situation, and only the goalkeeper has limited hands in the penalty box.

Players from both teams go through the normal process without any infractions prohibited by the regulations such as the action of holding the opponent (holding), the act of touching the ball using the hand (handball), the action of pushing the opponent (pushing), and offside. The goal must be scored by inserting the ball 100% into the goal line drawn on the opponent's goal line using all body parts except the arm, and the number of goals scored by both teams is compared to determine the winner.
