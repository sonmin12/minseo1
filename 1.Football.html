definition

One of the athletic events. The team consists of 11 people and should not use their hands except the goalkeeper, and uses the foot and head to score the opponent's goal. Both teams in the game have 11 players on the field. All players except the goalkeeper cannot use their hands except for the drawing situation, and only the goalkeeper has limited hands in the penalty box.
